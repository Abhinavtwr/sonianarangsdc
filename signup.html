About the Exclusive Consultation Program

We fix your relationship with food, health, and self because we believe that every body is unique inside and out, and there is no copy-paste solution or a one-size-fits-all. That’s why we work by crafting a personalised plan tailored to you. We educate you about what suits your body and make sure you are in the best shape while being happy, healthy and non-restrictive. We empower you to nourish your body, not just for the duration of the program, but for good.

from Delhi

Our premier consultation program is designed to be followed from across the globe. The appointments happen over skype/facetime/phone as per your convenience.

We Work With Everyone

We have been in this profession for over 28 years, catering to clients from all over the globe. From students to CEO’s to politicians to artists to homemakers to school teachers, from those looking simply for weight loss, to those struggling with PCOD, Hypothyroid, to diabetics, heart patients, and cancer fighters, we have a diverse clientel. Because you deserve more than just a diet, you deserve to see the potential of your body while being healthy and happy in all aspects of your life.

How it works

It’s a 3-stage process:

  • 1

    The Pre-Consultation Phase

    - You fill the ‘Getting to know you’ form to help us figure out your existing lifestyle, eating pattern, workout routine, sleep pattern, medical history and much more. As well as your goals and the expectations from the program.

    - A Whatsapp group is made that would include You, Your Personal Nutritionist, Sonia Narang, and the Front Desk Manager.

    - An appointment schedule is generated for the duration of the program based on your time and day preferences.

  • 2

    THE CONSULTATION PHASE

    - You fill the ‘Getting to know you’ form to help us figure out your existing lifestyle, eating pattern, workout routine, sleep pattern, medical history and much more. As well as your goals and the expectations from the program.

    - A Whatsapp group is made that would include You, Your Personal Nutritionist, Sonia Narang, and the Front Desk Manager.

    - An appointment schedule is generated for the duration of the program based on your time and day preferences.

  • 3

    THE POST-CONSULTATION PHASE

    - We maintain a lifelong relation with you and provide support anytime you need

    - A post-consultation document summarizing all the learnings from the program and also practical guidelines for most of the situations you will face going forward.
